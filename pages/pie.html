Grandma's Lemon Meringue Pie

On 9 inch pie
30 min - prep time
10 min - cook time
40 min - total
8 servings

Incredients

  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 3 tablespoons cornstarch
  • ¼ teaspoon salt
  • 1 ½ cups water
  • 2 lemons, juiced and zested
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 4 egg yolks, beaten
  • 1 (9 inch) pie crust, baked

Directions

Step 1 Gather all ingredients and preheat the oven to 325 degrees F (162 degrees C).
Step 2 To make the filling: Whisk 1 cup sugar, flour, cornstarch, and salt together in a medium saucepan; stir in water, lemon juice, and lemon zest. Cook over medium-high heat, stirring frequently, until mixture comes to a boil. Stir in butter.
Step 3 Place egg yolks in a small bowl and gradually whisk in 1/2 cup of hot sugar mixture.
Step 4 Whisk egg yolk mixture back into remaining sugar mixture. Bring to a boil and continue to cook while stirring constantly until thick. Remove from heat; pour filling into baked pastry shell.
Step 5 To make the meringue: Beat egg whites in a glass, metal, or ceramic bowl with an electric mixer until foamy. Gradually add sugar, continuing to beat until stiff peaks form.
Step 6 Spread meringue over pie filling, sealing the edges at the crust.
Step 7 Bake in preheated oven until meringue is golden brown, about 20 to 25 minutes.
Step 8 Serve and enjoy!
